OMNI: Your Personal Finance Assistant
Empowering your financial independence
Watch Our Video for a Quick Tour
OMNI is a secure application in which to undertake full personal finance management.
The complete financial overview. Details of all your assets and liabilities with associated transactional history and balances are contained within the single application, displayed to you in the most user friendly way. Link your assets: visual representation of assets icons. Link your liabilities: visual representation of liabilities icons. No other application offers such a far reaching and transparent view of your personal financial health whilst simultaneously saving you time and money whilst easing your frustration. Whether you are a user with a single bank account, a credit card and rent to pay or an experienced investor with a multitude of investments and assets, OMNI will give you clarity and control of your financial position.
